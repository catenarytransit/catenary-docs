# São Paulo

Catenary is a transit app that helps you see where your bus or train is, anywhere in São Paulo and its suburbs.  

[**Launch Catenary**](https://maps.catenarymaps.org/#pos=11.75/-23.5477/-46.6311)

## Supported agencies

With Catenary, you can see all of the bus routes and train lines operated by the following agencies, view upcoming departures, and view their timetables. 

* Rail system
  * Metrô-SP
  * ViaQuatro
  * ViaMobilidade
  * CPTM
* SPTrans buses

This is all of the agencies we know of that operate in São Paulo. The data for all operators is provided by SPTrans.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does Catenary show real-time data in São Paulo?

Unfortunately, no. The available data for buses is not in a format we can use, and real-time data is not available for trains.

### Does Catenary show service alerts in São Paulo?

Unfortunately, no. The available data is not in a format we can use.

### Do I need to download an app?
No. Catenary runs directly in your browser — just click [**Launch Catenary**](https://maps.catenarymaps.org/#pos=11.75/-23.5477/-46.6311).

### Is Catenary free to use?
Yes. It’s completely free and works on mobile or desktop.

### When does the next bus/train arrive?

[**Launch Catenary**](https://maps.catenarymaps.org/#pos=11.75/-23.5477/-46.6311) and search for your stop, or zoom in to find it. After clicking on the stop, the next arrivals will show up.

### Which bus stop or train station is closest to me?

[**Launch Catenary**](https://maps.catenarymaps.org/#pos=11.75/-23.5477/-46.6311) and your current location will appear. You can browse the map to find the nearest stop.
