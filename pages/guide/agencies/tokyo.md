## Rail operators

Please note that most operators will withdraw their data in January 2026, due to the end of the [ODPT Open Data Challenge 2025](https://challenge2025.odpt.org/en/outline.html). Additionally, through services between different operators are not supported.

* Yokohama Municipal Subway
* Kyoto City Subway
* JR East
* Tobu
* Sotetsu
* MIR Tsukuba Express
* Toei Subway
* TWR Rinkai Line
* Tokyo Metro
* Tama-Toshi Monorail

### Unsupported operators

* Keikyu
* Keio
* Keisei
* Odakyu
* Seibu
* Tokyu
* Yurikamome
* Toyo Rapid Railway
* Saitama Rapid Railway

and many small operators.

## Bus operators

We support many bus operators, including most of [the bus operators with GTFS data provided by ODPT](https://ckan.odpt.org/dataset/?_tags_limit=0&tags=%E3%83%90%E3%82%B9-bus&res_format=GTFS%2FGTFS-JP).
