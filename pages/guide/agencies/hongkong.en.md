# Hong Kong

We support buses and trams, but do not support MTR or light rail. We do not have realtime data. We will work on expanding support in the future; there are other open-source applications working on building high-quality data from the low-quality data that is currently available.

## How to help out

If you want to improve our data/maps, [join our Discord](https://discord.gg/bBeDhrzSgz) and head to one of the following threads/channels:

* [#general](https://discord.com/channels/1148092048625385552/1148092049413918772) - for problems unrelated to the specific agency
* [Hong Kong](https://discord.com/channels/1148092048625385552/1437142332259565748)
