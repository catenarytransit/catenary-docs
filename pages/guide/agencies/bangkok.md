# Bangkok

Catenary is a transit app that helps you see where your bus or train is, anywhere in Bangkok and the Île-de-France.  

[**Launch Catenary**](https://maps.catenarymaps.org/#pos=12.18/13.76766/100.5461)

## Supported agencies

With Catenary, you can see all of the bus routes and train lines operated by the following agencies, view upcoming departures, and view their timetables. Unfortunately, we do not have any realtime data.

All [data is provided by the Namtang API of the Office of Transport and Traffic Policy and Planning](https://www.transit.land/feeds/f-th~namtang).

### Rail

* **State Railway of Thailand** trains, including Red Lines and Airport Rail Link, are supported.
* **BTS Skytrain** trains are supported.
* **Bangkok MRT** trains are supported.

### Bus

We support bus operators whose [data is provided by the Namtang API of the Office of Transport and Traffic Policy and Planning](https://www.transit.land/feeds/f-th~namtang).

## Frequently asked questions

### Does Catenary show real-time data in Bangkok?

Unfortunately, no. We were not able to find a source of realtime data.

### Does Catenary show service alerts in Bangkok?

Unfortunately, no. We were not able to find a source of service alerts.

### Do I need to download an app?
No. Catenary runs directly in your browser — just click [**Launch Catenary**](https://maps.catenarymaps.org/#pos=12.18/13.76766/100.5461).

### Is Catenary free to use?
Yes. It’s completely free and works on mobile or desktop.

### When does the next bus/train arrive?

[**Launch Catenary**](https://maps.catenarymaps.org/#pos=12.18/13.76766/100.5461) and search for your stop, or zoom in to find it. After clicking on the stop, the next arrivals will show up.

### Which bus stop or train station is closest to me?

[**Launch Catenary**](https://maps.catenarymaps.org/#pos=12.18/13.76766/100.5461) and your current location will appear. You can browse the map to find the nearest stop.
